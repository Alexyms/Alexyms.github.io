About

I've led a $1B field failure program through a 50-person cross-factory organization built on influence without formal authority, turned a factory bottleneck into $473M in recovered annual capacity, and transformed an R&D team from zero product launches to three.

The throughline is strategic problem identification and cross-boundary execution. I see system topology — both technical and organizational — faster than most, and I translate across the interfaces where complexity lives: between design and manufacturing, between engineering and executive leadership, between architecture and implementation. The technical depth is real, but it serves the strategic thinking, not the other way around.
